  • streamURL

    A streamURL that is compatible on Apple devices (can be nil) The streamURLs may contain both video and audio streams, some video streams do not contain any audio. This property will return a video stream that contains both audio and video with a maximum video quality of 720p in the case of videos that aren’t live. Also, this properly will return the URL to a live stream in the case of live videos.
